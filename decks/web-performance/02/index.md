# Why Performance Matters

## The Business Impact

| Metric | Impact | Source |
|--------|--------|--------|
| 53% | Mobile users abandon sites taking >3s to load | Google |
| 7% | Conversion drops with just 1s delay | Aberdeen Group |
| 16% | Decrease in customer satisfaction per 100ms delay | Akamai |
| 2x | Mobile revenue increase after 0.1s improvement | Walmart |

## SEO Impact
- Core Web Vitals are a **Google ranking factor** since 2021
- Faster sites rank higher in search results
- Performance affects crawl budget for large sites

## User Experience
- **Perceived performance** matters as much as actual speed
- Loading states and progress indicators improve satisfaction
- Progressive enhancement ensures basic functionality works immediately

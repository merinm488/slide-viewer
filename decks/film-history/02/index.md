## Key Eras

### Silent Era (1910s-1920s)
- **Charlie Chaplin** - The Tramp character
- **Buster Keaton** - Stone-face physical comedy
- **Harold Lloyd** - Thrilling stunt sequences
- **D.W. Griffith** - Birth of a Nation (technical milestone)

### Transition to Talkies (1927-1931)
- **The Jazz Singer** (1927) - First feature-length "talkie"
- **Lights of New York** (1928) - First all-talking feature
- Many silent film stars struggled with voice acting

### The Golden Age (1930s-1950s)
- **Hays Code** - Strict moral content guidelines
- **Star System** - Actors under exclusive contracts
- **Vertical Integration** - Studios controlled production, distribution, and exhibition

# What is the National Budget?

A national budget is the government's financial plan outlining how public funds will be collected and spent over the coming year.

**Purpose of the Budget**

- **Resource Allocation**: Distributes funds across sectors like healthcare, education, defense, and infrastructure
- **Economic Steering**: Uses fiscal policy to influence economic growth and employment
- **Public Accountability**: Shows citizens how their tax dollars are being used
- **Policy Implementation**: Funds the government's priorities and programs

**Key Components**

- **Revenue**: Income from taxes, duties, and other government earnings
- **Expenditure**: Planned spending on various government programs and services
- **Deficit/Surplus**: The gap between revenue and spending
- **Borrowing**: How the government plans to finance any deficit

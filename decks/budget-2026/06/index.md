# Impact & Way Forward

Budget 2026 aims to balance fiscal discipline with growth-oriented spending.

**Expected Benefits**

**For Individuals**

- Higher disposable income due to tax relief
- Better healthcare access through expanded schemes
- Improved education and skill opportunities

**For Businesses**

- Reduced compliance burden with simplified tax system
- Incentives for manufacturing and startups
- Enhanced infrastructure supporting business operations

**For the Economy**

- Stimulated demand through increased public spending
- Job creation through infrastructure and manufacturing focus
- Sustainable growth with green investments

**Key Takeaways**

- **Fiscal Consolidation**: On track to meet deficit targets
- **Growth-Oriented**: Capital expenditure at record highs
- **Inclusive Focus**: Social sector allocations increased
- **Future-Ready**: Emphasis on digital and green economy

**Monitor These Metrics**

- GDP growth rate trajectory
- Tax collection vs. targets
- Infrastructure project completion
- Employment generation numbers

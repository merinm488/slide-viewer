# Budget 2026: Key Highlights

This year's budget introduces several significant changes aimed at strengthening the economy and supporting citizens.

**Major Announcements**

- **Economic Growth Target**: 6.5-7% GDP growth projected for FY 2026
- **Tax Reform**: New tax slabs with increased exemption limits for middle-class taxpayers
- **Infrastructure Push**: Record allocation of ₹11 lakh crore for capital expenditure
- **Digital India**: Enhanced funding for technology initiatives and digital public infrastructure

**Focus Areas for 2026**

1. **Inclusive Growth**: Programs targeting rural development and employment generation
2. **Green Transition**: Increased spending on renewable energy and sustainability projects
3. **Healthcare Expansion**: Higher allocation for public health and insurance schemes
4. **Education & Skills**: Investment in new IITs, IIMs, and skill development programs

**Deficit Management**

- **Fiscal Deficit Target**: 5.1% of GDP, down from 5.8% in previous year
- **Disinvestment Target**: ₹50,000 crore through strategic stake sales

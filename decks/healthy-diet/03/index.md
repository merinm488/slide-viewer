# Micronutrients & Hydration

## Essential Vitamins

| Vitamin | Function | Best Sources |
|---------|----------|--------------|
| **A** | Vision, immune system | Carrots, sweet potatoes, spinach |
| **B Complex** | Energy metabolism | Whole grains, meat, eggs |
| **C** | Immune, collagen | Citrus, bell peppers, broccoli |
| **D** | Bone health, immunity | Sunlight, fatty fish, fortified dairy |
| **E** | Antioxidant, skin health | Nuts, seeds, vegetable oils |
| **K** | Blood clotting | Leafy greens, broccoli |

## Essential Minerals

| Mineral | Function | Best Sources |
|---------|----------|--------------|
| **Calcium** | Bones, teeth | Dairy, leafy greens, fortified foods |
| **Iron** | Red blood cells | Red meat, beans, spinach (with vit C) |
| **Magnesium** | Muscle/nerve function | Nuts, seeds, whole grains |
| **Potassium** | Heart function, fluid balance | Bananas, potatoes, avocados |
| **Zinc** | Immune function, wound healing | Oysters, beef, pumpkin seeds |

## Hydration

**Daily water intake:**
| Factor | Recommended |
|--------|-------------|
| General guideline | 8 cups (2L) per day |
| Exercise | +1-2 cups per hour |
| Hot weather | +1-2 cups per hour |
| Altitude | Increase intake |

**Signs of dehydration:**
- Dark yellow urine
- Dry mouth/thirst
- Fatigue
- Headache
- Dizziness

**Tips for staying hydrated:**
- Carry a water bottle
- Set reminders throughout the day
- Eat water-rich foods (cucumber, watermelon)
- Limit caffeine and alcohol

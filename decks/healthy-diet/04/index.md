# Exercise & Movement

## Types of Exercise

| Type | Description | Frequency | Examples |
|------|-------------|------------|----------|
| **Cardio** | Improves heart health | 150 min/week moderate | Running, swimming, cycling |
| **Strength** | Builds muscle, bone density | 2-3x/week | Weightlifting, resistance bands |
| **Flexibility** | Mobility, injury prevention | Daily | Stretching, yoga |
| **Balance** | Fall prevention, stability | 2-3x/week | Tai chi, single-leg stands |

## Cardio Exercise Guidelines

| Intensity | Heart Rate | Talk Test | Examples |
|-----------|------------|-----------|----------|
| **Light** | 50-60% max | Can sing | Walking, light housework |
| **Moderate** | 60-70% max | Can talk, not sing | Brisk walking, cycling |
| **Vigorous** | 70-85% max | Can't say more than few words | Running, HIIT, swimming |

**Target Heart Rate Formula:**
```
Max HR = 220 - age
Moderate zone = Max HR × 0.6 to 0.7
Vigorous zone = Max HR × 0.7 to 0.85
```

## Strength Training Basics

**Major muscle groups to target:**
1. **Chest** - Push-ups, bench press, chest fly
2. **Back** - Rows, pull-ups, lat pulldown
3. **Legs** - Squats, lunges, deadlifts
4. **Shoulders** - Overhead press, lateral raises
5. **Arms** - Bicep curls, tricep dips
6. **Core** - Planks, crunches, Russian twists

**Progressive Overload:**
- Start with bodyweight or light weights
- Gradually increase: weight, reps, or sets
- Rest 48 hours between training same muscle group
- Focus on form over weight

| Wednesday | Rest or light walk | 20 min |

| Friday | Cardio | 30 min |

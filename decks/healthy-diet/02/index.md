# Macronutrients Explained

## The Three Macros

| Macronutrient | Calories/gram | Primary Function | Daily % |
|---------------|---------------|------------------|---------|
| **Carbohydrates** | 4 | Primary energy source | 45-65% |
| **Protein** | 4 | Build & repair tissue | 10-35% |
| **Fats** | 9 | Long-term energy, hormones | 20-35% |

## Carbohydrates

| Type | Description | Examples |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Complex** | Slow-digesting, fiber-rich | Whole grains, vegetables, legumes |
| **Simple** | Quick energy, less fiber | Fruits, dairy, refined sugar |

**Choose whole grains** over refined:
- Brown rice, quinoa, oats
- Whole wheat bread
- Sweet potatoes
- Beans and lentils

## Proteins

| Source | Protein (per 100g) | Benefits |
|---------|-------------------|----------|
| Chicken breast | 31g | Lean, versatile |
| Salmon | 20g + omega-3 | Heart-healthy fats |
| Eggs | 13g | Complete protein |
| Lentils | 9g | Fiber + protein |
| Greek yogurt | 10g | Probiotics |

## Fats

| Type | Sources | Recommendation |
|------|---------|----------------|
| **Unsaturated** | Olive oil, nuts, avocado, fish | ✅ Choose these |
| **Saturated** | Butter, red meat, coconut oil | Limit intake |
| **Trans** | Processed foods, fried foods | ❌ Avoid |

**Healthy fat sources:** Avocados, nuts, seeds, olive oil, fatty fish

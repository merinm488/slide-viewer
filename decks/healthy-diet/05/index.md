# Healthy Habits & Meal Planning

## Daily Habits for Success

| Habit | Why It Matters | How to Start |
|-------|----------------|--------------|
| **Eat breakfast** | Kickstarts metabolism | Overnight oats, eggs, smoothie |
| **Meal prep** | Avoids unhealthy choices | Prep Sunday for the week |
| **Eat mindfully** | Better digestion, satisfaction | No screens while eating |
| **Sleep 7-9 hours** | Recovery, hormone balance | Set consistent bedtime |
| **Manage stress** | Reduces cortisol (belly fat) | Meditation, walks, breathing |

## Building a Balanced Plate

### The Perfect Plate Formula:

| Portion | Food Group | Examples |
|----------|------------|----------|
| **50%** | Vegetables | Broccoli, spinach, peppers, carrots |
| **25%** | Lean Protein | Chicken, fish, tofu, eggs, beans |
| **25%** | Whole Grains | Brown rice, quinoa, oats, sweet potato |
| **Small** | Healthy Fats | Avocado, olive oil, nuts, seeds |

### Visual Guide:
```
Half plate = Colorful vegetables
Quarter plate = Quality protein
Quarter plate = Complex carbs
Side = Healthy fats
```

## Meal Prep Tips

### Weekly Prep Strategy:
1. **Pick 2-3 proteins** - Chicken, salmon, tofu
2. **Choose 3-4 vegetables** - Broccoli, bell peppers, sweet potatoes
3. **Select 2-3 grains** - Brown rice, quinoa, farro
4. **Prep components** - Cook everything separately
5. **Mix & match** - Create different meals all week

### Sample Prep Menu:

| Meal | Components |
|------|------------|
| Breakfast | Overnight oats + berries + nuts |
| Lunch | Grilled chicken + quinoa + roasted veggies |
| Dinner | Salmon + sweet potato + asparagus |
| Snacks | Greek yogurt, apple + almond butter, hummus + veggies |

## Foods to Limit

| Category | Examples | Healthier Alternatives |
|----------|----------|------------------------|
| **Added sugars** | Soda, candy, pastries | Fruit, dark chocolate (70%+) |
| **Processed meats** | Bacon, sausage, hot dogs | Chicken, turkey, fish |
| **Refined carbs** | White bread, sugary cereal | Whole grains, oats |
| **Fried foods** | French fries, fried chicken | Baked, grilled, air-fried |
| **Alcohol** | Beer, wine, cocktails | Sparkling water with lime |

**Remember:** 80/20 rule - eat healthy 80% of the time, enjoy treats 20% of the time!

# Classical Foundations

## Orchestral Periods

| Period | Years | Characteristics | Key Composers |
|--------|-------|----------------|---------------|
| **Baroque** | 1600-1750 | Ornate, complex, polyphonic | Bach, Vivaldi, Handel |
| **Classical** | 1750-1820 | Elegant, balanced, structured | Mozart, Haydn, early Beethoven |
| **Romantic** | 1800-1900 | Emotional, expressive, larger | Beethoven, Chopin, Tchaikovsky |
| **Modern** | 1900-present | Experimental, diverse | Stravinsky, Schoenberg |

## Essential Compositions

### Baroque Masterpieces
- **Bach** - Brandenburg Concertos, Well-Tempered Clavier
- **Vivaldi** - The Four Seasons
- **Handel** - Messiah, Water Music

### Classical Era
- **Mozart** - Symphony No. 40, The Magic Flute, Requiem
- **Haydn** - Surprise Symphony, The Creation
- **Beethoven (early)** - Symphony No. 1-2, Piano Sonatas

### Romantic Period
- **Beethoven (late)** - Symphony No. 5, 9, Moonlight Sonata
- **Chopin** - Nocturnes, Études, Preludes
- **Tchaikovsky** - Swan Lake, 1812 Overture, Symphony No. 6
- **Brahms** - Hungarian Dances, Lullaby
- **Dvořák** - New World Symphony

## Instrument Families

| Family | Instruments | Role |
|--------|-------------|------|
| **Strings** | Violin, viola, cello, bass | Melody & harmony |
| **Woodwinds** | Flute, oboe, clarinet, bassoon | Color & texture |
| **Brass** | Trumpet, horn, trombone, tuba | Power & emphasis |
| **Percussion** | Timpani, snare, cymbals | Rhythm & effects |

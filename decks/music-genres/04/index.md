# Rock Revolution

## The British Invasion (1960s)

| Band | Year Arrived in US | Impact | Iconic Songs |
|------|-------------------|--------|--------------|
| **The Beatles** | 1964 | Changed everything | Hey Jude, Let It Be |
| **The Rolling Stones** | 1964 | Raw, rebellious energy | Satisfaction, Paint It Black |
| **The Who** | 1967 | Power chords, destruction | My Generation, Baba O'Riley |
| **Led Zeppelin** | 1969 | Hard rock pioneers | Stairway to Heaven, Kashmir |
| **Pink Floyd** | 1967 | Psychedelic masterpieces | Wish You Were Here, Comfortably Numb |

## Rock Evolution Timeline

| Era | Years | Characteristics | Key Albums |
|------|-------|----------------|------------|
| **Classic Rock** | 60s-70s | Guitar-driven, extended solos | Led Zeppelin IV, Dark Side of the Moon |
| **Punk** | 70s | Fast, aggressive, DIY | Never Mind the Bollocks, Ramones |
| **New Wave** | late 70s-80s | Synthesizers, art-pop | Remain in Light, Speaking and Spelling |
| **Grunge** | 90s | Heavy guitars, angst | Nevermind, Ten, Superunknown |
| **Alternative** | 90s-present | Diverse, non-mainstream | OK Computer, Is This It |

## Rock Subgenres

| Subgenre | Characteristics | Notable Bands |
|----------|----------------|---------------|
| **Prog Rock** | Complex, long compositions | Yes, King Crimson, Genesis |
| **Metal** | Heavy distortion, power chords | Black Sabbath, Metallica, Iron Maiden |
| **Punk** | Fast, short, anti-establishment | Sex Pistols, The Clash, Dead Kennedys |
| **Grunge** | Heavy guitars, sludgy production | Nirvana, Pearl Jam, Soundgarden |
| **Indie Rock** | Lo-fi, DIY, diverse | Pixies, Sonic Youth, Arcade Fire |
| **Stoner Rock** | Slow, heavy, psychedelic | Queens of the Stone Age, Kyuss |

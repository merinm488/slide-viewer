# Getting Started Checklist

You've learned the fundamentals of stock market investing. Now it's time to take action. Here's your practical checklist to begin your investing journey.

## Before You Invest

**1. Build Your Foundation**
- [ ] Pay off high-interest debt (credit cards above 7-8%)
- [ ] Establish an emergency fund (3-6 months expenses)
- [ ] Set clear financial goals

**2. Know Your Numbers**
- [ ] Determine your risk tolerance
- [ ] Decide your asset allocation
- [ ] Set your investment budget

**3. Choose Your Account**
- [ ] Select a brokerage firm
- [ ] Open your investment account
- [ ] Complete any required verification

## Taking Your First Steps

**Week 1: Get Set Up**
- [ ] Open and fund your brokerage account
- [ ] Research your first investments (start with index funds)
- [ ] Set up automatic transfers if possible

**Week 2: Make Your First Purchase**
- [ ] Buy your first stock or fund
- [ ] Start with a small amount if you're nervous
- [ ] Set up a watchlist for future investments

**Ongoing: Build Habits**
- [ ] Set up monthly automatic investments
- [ ] Schedule quarterly portfolio reviews
- [ ] Continue learning about investing

## Final Tips for Success

- Start early—even small amounts grow over time
- Stay consistent—automate your investments
- Think long term—ignore short-term noise
- Keep learning—read books, follow reputable sources
- Avoid panic—market downturns are normal

> The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now.

**Your investing journey begins today. Good luck!**

# American Sports Giants

## NFL (National Football League)

| Super Bowl | Viewers | Winning Team |
|------------|---------|--------------|
| LV (2021) | 96M+ | Tampa Bay Buccaneers |
| LIV (2022) | 99M+ | Kansas City Chiefs |
| LVII (2023) | 113M+ | Kansas City Chiefs |

**Key Facts:**
- 32 teams, 17-week regular season
- Super Bowl is the most-watched U.S. TV event annually
- Average career length: ~3.5 years

## NBA (National Basketball Association)

| Conference | Current Dominant Teams |
|------------|----------------------|
| Eastern | Boston, Milwaukee, Philadelphia |
| Western | Denver, Phoenix, Golden State |

**Global Reach:**
- Players from 40+ countries
- China is largest market outside U.S.
- 82-game regular season

**All-Time Greats:**
- Michael Jordan - 6 championships, 5 MVPs
- LeBron James - All-time leading scorer
- Kareem Abdul-Jabbar - 6 MVPs record

## MLB (Major League Baseball)

**Statistics:**
- 162 games per team (longest season)
- 30 teams across U.S. and Canada
- "America's Pastime" since 1869

**Iconic Records:**
| Record | Player | Year |
|--------|--------|------|
| Most HR in season | Barry Bonds (73) | 2001 |
| 56-game hitting streak | Joe DiMaggio | 1941 |
| Most career HR | Barry Bonds (762) | 2007 |
| Perfect games | 23 in MLB history | - |

## NHL (National Hockey League)
- Stanley Cup - oldest trophy in North American sports (1893)
- 7-game series in playoffs
- Growing rapidly in U.S. sunbelt markets

# Biodiversity & Classification

## Biodiversity by the Numbers

| Category | Estimated Species | Described Species |
|----------|-------------------|-------------------|
| **Insects** | 5-10 million | ~1 million |
| **Mammals** | ~6,400 | ~5,500 |
| **Birds** | ~10,000-11,000 | ~10,000 |
| **Reptiles** | ~8,700 | ~11,000 |
| **Amphibians** | ~8,000 | ~7,000 |
| **Fish** | ~35,000 | ~34,000 |

## Animal Classification

| Group | Characteristics | Examples |
|-------|----------------|----------|
| **Mammals** | Warm-blooded, hair/fur, milk | Lions, whales, humans |
| **Birds** | Feathers, lay eggs, most fly | Eagles, penguins, ostriches |
| **Reptiles** | Cold-blooded, scales | Snakes, turtles, crocodiles |
| **Amphibians** | Cold-blooded, skin needs moisture | Frogs, salamanders |
| **Fish** | Gills, aquatic | Sharks, clownfish |
| **Insects** | Exoskeleton, 6 legs | Ants, butterflies |
| **Arachnids** | Exoskeleton, 8 legs | Spiders, scorpions |

**Fun fact:** We've only discovered about 20% of all species on Earth!

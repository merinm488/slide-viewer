# Ocean Giants & Marine Life

## Marine Mammals

| Species | Length | Weight | Lifespan | Diet |
|---------|--------|--------|----------|-------|
| **Blue Whale** | 100 ft (30m) | 200 tons | 80-90 yrs | Krill |
| **Orca** | 26 ft (8m) | 6 tons | 50-80 yrs | Fish, marine mammals |
| **Humpback Whale** | 50 ft (15m) | 40 tons | 45-50 yrs | Krill, small fish |
| **Sperm Whale** | 52 ft (16m) | 45 tons | 70 yrs | Giant squid |
| **Dolphin** | 13 ft (4m) | 1,400 lbs | 40-60 yrs | Fish, squid |

## Blue Whale Facts
- **Largest animal ever** (bigger than dinosaurs!)
- Heart size = small car
- Tongue weighs as much as an elephant
- Can hear each other from **1,000 miles away**

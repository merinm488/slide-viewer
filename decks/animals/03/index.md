# Big Cats: Apex Predators

## Species Overview

| Species | Habitat | Status | Top Speed |
|---------|---------|--------|-----------|
| **Lion** | African savanna | Vulnerable | 50 mph (80 km/h) |
| **Tiger** | Asian forests | Endangered | 40 mph (65 km/h) |
| **Leopard** | Africa & Asia | Vulnerable | 36 mph (58 km/h) |
| **Jaguar** | Americas | Near Threatened | 50 mph (80 km/h) |
| **Snow Leopard** | Central Asia mountains | Vulnerable | 40 mph (65 km/h) |
| **Cheetah** | African savanna | Vulnerable | 70 mph (112 km/h) |

## Fun Facts

### Lions
- Only cats that live in groups called **prides**
- A pride's roar can be heard from **5 miles away**
- Males' manes grow darker with age (sign of dominance)
- Sleep up to **20 hours a day**

### Tigers
- **Largest cats** - up to 660 lbs (300 kg)
- Each tiger has **unique stripe pattern** (like fingerprints)
- Excellent swimmers (unlike most cats)

### Cheetahs
- **Fastest land animal** - 0-60 mph in 3 seconds
- Can only sustain top speed for ~30 seconds
- Semi-retractable claws (like dogs) for traction
- Distinctive "tear marks" reduce glare

## Conservation Status

| Status | Meaning | Big Cats Affected |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| **Endangered** | Very high risk | Tiger |
| **Vulnerable** | High risk | Lion, Leopard, Snow Leopard |
| **Near Threatened** | Likely to become endangered | Jaguar |

**Major threats:** Habitat loss, poaching, human-wildlife conflict

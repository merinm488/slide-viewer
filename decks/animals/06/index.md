## Conservation Status

| Species | Status | Population | Main Threat |
|---------|--------|------------|-------------|
| **Amur Leopard** | Critically Endangered | ~100 in wild | Habitat loss, poaching |
| **Vaquita** | Critically Endangered | ~10 remaining | Fishing nets |
| **Mountain Gorilla** | Endangered | ~1,000 | Poaching, habitat |
| **Blue Whale** | Endangered | 10,000-25,000 | Ship strikes, climate |
| **Sea Turtle** | Various | Species-dependent | Plastic, habitat loss |

## Conservation Success Stories

| Species | Then | Now | What Worked |
|---------|-----|-----|-------------|
| **Bald Eagle** | Endangered (1963) | Recovered (2007) | DDT ban, habitat protection |
| **Giant Panda** | Endangered (1990) | Vulnerable (2016) | Habitat protection, breeding |
| **Gray Whale** | Endangered (1970) | Recovered (1994) | Whaling ban |
| **Arabian Oryx** | Extinct in wild (1972) | Reintroduced | Captive breeding |
